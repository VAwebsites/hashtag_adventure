Bungy Jumping

Bungy Jumping and Extreme Adventure Sports in Rishikesh with Jumpin Heights. The activity is performed in Mohan Chatti village near Rishikesh. As they call it “ Got Guts? ”, Yes, they call it for a reason!! Bungee jumping in Rishikesh is India’s highest bungee jumping point i.e., around 83 meters in height. The activity is performed in Mohan Chatti village near Rishikesh. As the name (Bungee Jumping) suggests, it involves jumping from a fixed cantilever with a rubber cord tied on your ankle and the face facing towards the rocky river HALL. This setup is situated between a valley which itself gives it an adventurous and thrilling look. Surely talking about such an activity, the first thing come in people’s mind would be safety. The Bungee jumping in Rishikesh is set up only by giving priority to the safety of the jumpers. So the whole activity is guided and performed under the professional masters who are specially flown in from New Zealand to Rishikesh, to operate such an tremendous art.
